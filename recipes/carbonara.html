Carbonara

Recipe for a traditional Italian carbonara

This is a recipe taught to me by my dad, an Italian chef

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Spaghetti
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add salt and water to a pan, bring to boil
  2. While water is boiling, cut Guanciale into small cubes
  3. Add Guanciale to a frying pan on medium heat (no oil needed) and regulalry stir
  4. Add Spaghetti to boiling water and cook for 6 minutes
  5. While Guanciale and Spaghetti cook, mix 2 egg yolks, 1 whole egg, Pecorino cheese, Parmesan cheese and pepper
  6. Once Guanciale and Speghetti are cooked add Saghetti to the Guanciale frying pan
  7. Add a generous amount of water and increase heat, continously stir to release the starch from the pasta
  8. Once pasta is cooked, turn off the heat and add the egg and cheese mix
  9. Mix in well and serve
